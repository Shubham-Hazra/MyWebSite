CHESS


One of my favourite things to do in free time is to play chess. I used to play chess a lot as a child but then for some reason I lost touch with it, but recently with a huge boom of chess in the youtube industry, mainly because of Samay Raina, I picked it up again and am once again enjoying playing this marvellous ancient game. I also like watching chess youtubers like GothamChess and Agadmator. I like to try out new openings and am more of a tactical player. I mostly played online on sites like chess.com or lichess, however recently my wingmates have also started playing and we play together on this beautiful wooden board that I have. I would very much like to call myself a good player but that's just not true, however I do know that I enjoy playing this game and I hope to get much better at it.
